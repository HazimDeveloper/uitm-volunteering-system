Landslide in Batang Kali, Pahang

A landslide occurred in the early hours of 16 December 2022 near the Malaysian town of Batang Kali, Selangor, displacing 450,000 m3 (16 million cu ft) of soil and burying campsites at an organic farm. The accident trapped 92 people under the collapsed slope, most were campers from the farm. Thirty-one people were killed and 61 were rescued, with eight people requiring hospitalisation.
